Connects your EasyMe account to Wordpress.

== Description ==

Connects your EasyMe account via OAuth2 and automatically embeds your custom javascript client code in your Web site.

"Magic" EasyMe links are still created in your EasyMe account under "settings" > "integration".

The plugin will automatically update your embedded code, so once connected, you can forget about it.

== Installation ==

To connect: Simply navigate to "Tools" > "EasyMe Connect", click the button and authorize the connection in your EasyMe account.

To disconnect: Simply de-activate the plugin.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I insert sales links from EasyMe in my Wordpress page? =

You pull the links you need from your EasyMe account - go to "Settings" > "Integrations" and get the links from part 3) in the widget

= Can I insert clients from multiple EasyMe accounts in the same Wordpress installation? =

Not at this time.

Questions answered by the sublime support at support@easyme.biz
